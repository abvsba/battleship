- Step 0: Game rules Welcome to a BattleShip Game. Here are some steps you can follow to start the game.


- Step 1: Positioning ships You can position the ships within the table, ensuring they do not overlap. Once all the ships are positioned correctly, you can start the game.


- Step 2: Starting game Once the game has started, you can click on a cell of the enemy board to launch a missile.


- Step 3: Processing game Keep launching missiles until you sink all the ships to win the game. But be careful, if the enemy sinks all your ships first, you lose the game!
